About this game

Jewels Charm: Match 3 Game Pro - A Puzzle Adventure Full of Magic and Strategy!

Immerse yourself in the colorful world of Jewels Charm, a match-3 adventure where every move counts. This game blends puzzle-solving mechanics with strategic planning, creating a captivating and immersive experience.

  • Unlock Special Items by mastering unique jewel combinations like the Lightning Jewel that blasts entire rows or the Jeweled Bomb that clears nearby jewels.
  • Take on hundreds of levels featuring various objectives such as gathering stars, eliminating obstacles, and unlocking hidden treasures.
  • Master the timing mechanics to extend gameplay with the Timing Jewel, giving you extra moves when played wisely.
  • Complete puzzles to unlock new maps, reveal stunning environments, and gather precious jewels.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a dedicated puzzle enthusiast, Jewels Charm offers the perfect blend of fun, challenge, and brain-training puzzles.
